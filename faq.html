Booking Questions

To book tickets:

  1. Select the movie or event you want to attend
  2. Choose your preferred date, time, and venue
  3. Select your seats (for movies and reserved seating events)
  4. Proceed to payment and complete your booking
  5. You'll receive a confirmation email with your e-tickets

Cancellation and modification policies vary:

  • Movies: Cancellation allowed up to 1 hour before showtime with partial refund
  • Events: Depends on organizer's policy (check event details)
  • Modifications: Seat upgrades may be available, date/time changes rarely allowed

Visit "My Bookings" in your account to check options.

We offer multiple delivery options:

  • E-Ticket: Instantly delivered via email and available in "My Bookings"
  • Mobile Ticket: Accessible in our mobile app (recommended for quick entry)
  • Box Office Pickup: Collect at venue with booking ID and ID proof (for some events)

Payment Questions

We accept various payment options:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, etc.)
  • Net Banking
  • UPI Payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
  • Digital Wallets (Paytm, Amazon Pay, etc.)
  • BookMyShow Gift Cards
  • Cash at Partner Outlets (select locations)

Refund processing times vary by payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 7-10 business days
  • Net Banking: 3-5 business days
  • UPI & Wallets: 1-3 business days

Note: The refund timeline begins after the refund is approved, which may take 1-2 business days for verification.

In case of payment failure with amount deduction:

  1. Check your email for a payment confirmation/failure notification
  2. Wait 2 hours - most payments automatically reverse in this time
  3. If amount isn't reversed, contact your bank with the transaction details
  4. If the ticket was booked despite the failure message, check "My Bookings" in your account
  5. Contact our customer support with transaction details if issue persists

Note: The refund process may take 7-10 business days.

Account Questions

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter the email address or mobile number associated with your account
  4. Check your email or phone for a password reset link
  5. Click the link and create a new password

If you don't receive the reset email, check your spam folder or contact support.

To update your account details:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Click on your profile picture in the top right corner
  3. Select "Account Settings"
  4. Update your personal information, email, or phone number
  5. Click "Save Changes"

Note: Changing your email or phone number may require verification through a one-time password (OTP).

Yes, we take security very seriously:

  • All payment transactions are encrypted with 256-bit SSL encryption
  • We are PCI-DSS compliant (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard)
  • We don't store your full credit card details on our servers
  • Two-factor authentication is available for account security
  • Regular security audits are conducted to protect your data

For additional security, we recommend:

  • Using strong, unique passwords
  • Not sharing your account credentials
  • Logging out after each session on shared devices
